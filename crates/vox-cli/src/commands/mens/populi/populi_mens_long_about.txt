The Vox Mens subsystem handles everything related to AI model
training, inference serving, and training data pipelines.

Quick start:

  vox mens corpus extract .    # extract corpus from vox files
  vox mens corpus pairs ...    # generate training pairs
  vox mens train --model Qwen/Qwen2.5-Coder-3B-Instruct
  vox mens pipeline            # Corpus → eval → optional native train
  vox mens serve --model ...   # HTTP serve
